In Falling Pickaxe, your journey begins with a simple wooden pickaxe, but the goal is to reach maximum power. To evolve, you just need engagement: for every thousand likes on the stream, you earn an automatic upgrade. Want to speed things up? The Ice Cream gift gives you a level instantly, and the Finger Heart guarantees seven levels at once. 

You'll progress through stone, copper, gold, iron, and diamond until you reach the legendary Netherite. And the evolution doesn't stop there! Once your main pickaxe reaches max level, the next upgrade unlocks an auxiliary pickaxe to mine alongside you. You can unlock up to four auxiliaries, forming a true mining army. 

Best of all, they share your special skills: if you activate the giant pickaxe or the sword, all your auxiliaries receive the same power instantly. Show your strength and dominate the depths!
